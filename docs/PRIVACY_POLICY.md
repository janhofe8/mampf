# MAMPF Privacy Policy

_Last updated: 2026-04-24_

MAMPF ("we", "us") is a personal restaurant recommendation app for Hamburg, built by Jan Hoferichter. This policy explains what data we collect, why, and how to control it.

## What we collect

### If you create an account
- **Email address** — used to sign in and deliver account-related emails (e.g. confirmation, password reset). Stored by our auth provider Supabase.
- **Display name** (optional) — shown in your profile and used for personalized greetings. You can change or remove it anytime in "Edit Profile".
- **User ID** — a random identifier generated by Supabase to associate your ratings with your account.
- **Your restaurant ratings** (scores 1–10) — tied to your account so you can see them across devices.
- **Your favorites / "want to try" list** — currently stored locally on your device only.

### If you're not signed in
- **Anonymous device ID** — a random identifier stored locally so your ratings can be saved without an account. It is never combined with your name, email, or other personal data.
- **Your restaurant ratings** — tied to the anonymous device ID only.

### Location
- We request your location only **while you're using the app**, and only if you grant permission.
- It is used **client-side only** to:
  - Show your position on the map
  - Calculate distance to restaurants
- Your location is **never transmitted to our servers or any third party**.

## What we don't do
- **No tracking across apps or websites.** No advertising SDKs, no fingerprinting.
- **No analytics SDKs.** We don't know which screens you visit or how long you use the app.
- **No selling or sharing of personal data** with third parties for marketing.

## Third-party services we use
- **Supabase** (hosted in the EU) — PostgreSQL database + authentication. Stores your account, ratings, and favorites. Privacy: https://supabase.com/privacy
- **Google Places API** — used to fetch restaurant metadata (address, opening hours, public ratings). Restaurant data is requested by us on your behalf; Google does not receive your identity. Privacy: https://policies.google.com/privacy
- **Apple iCloud Keychain** (optional) — used to save your login if you opt in via the iOS "Save Password" prompt.

## Your rights and controls

You control your data at all times:

- **Edit your profile** — change your name or email anytime via Profile → tap your name.
- **Delete your account** — Profile → Settings → "Delete my account". This permanently removes your account, all ratings, all favorites, and cannot be undone.
- **Delete your data without deleting the account** — Profile → Settings → "Delete my data" while signed out (removes device-scoped anonymous ratings and resets the device ID).
- **Export your data** — contact us (see below) and we'll provide an export within 30 days.
- **Revoke location access** — iOS Settings → MAMPF → Location.

## Data retention
- **Account data:** retained until you delete your account.
- **Anonymous ratings (device ID):** retained until you tap "Delete my data" in Settings.
- **Backups** may retain copies for up to 30 days after deletion before being purged.

## Children
MAMPF is not directed at children under 13. We do not knowingly collect data from children.

## Changes to this policy
If we make material changes we'll update the "Last updated" date above and notify signed-in users in-app.
